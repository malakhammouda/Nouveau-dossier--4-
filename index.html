CDEC'S TEAMS

WEB DEVELOPEMENT

The Web Development Team is a creative and technical group , where members learn how to design and build modern websites Through workshops and collaborative projects.

APP DEVELOPEMENT

The App Development Team teaches members to design and build mobile or desktop apps. Through practical projects, they learn key programming skills and create innovative, real-world solutions.

VIDEO EDITING

The Video Editing Team teaches members how to create and edit videos professionally. They learn storytelling, effects, and editing tools to produce engaging visual content.

UI/UX

The UI/UX Design Team helps members learn how to design user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces. They focus on creativity, usability, and improving the overall user experience.

SECURITY

The Security Team introduces members to cybersecurity basics, teaching them how to protect systems, networks, and data through practical tools and ethical hacking concepts.
